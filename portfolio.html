Magpul MOE Stock

2025 Game Asset Weapon Components

Description

A highly detailed 3D model of the Magpul MOE stock, created for use in first-person shooter games. The model includes accurate dimensions and details based on reference images of the real-world product.

The texturing includes realistic wear patterns and material definition to match the polymer construction of the actual stock.

Technical Specifications

  • Software: Autodesk Maya, Substance Painter, Marmoset Toolbag 4
  • Polygons: 2,500 tris
  • Textures: 4K PBR (Albedo, Normal, Roughness, Metalness)
  • UV Maps: 1 UV channel
  • Rendered with: Marmoset Toolbag 4
